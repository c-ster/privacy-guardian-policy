Privacy Guardian 2 - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 30, 2025

Overview

Privacy Guardian 2 is a Chrome extension designed to protect your personal information by detecting and helping you redact Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Protected Health Information (PHI) from your interactions with Large Language Models (LLMs).

Our Commitment to Privacy

Privacy Guardian 2 is built with a local-first, privacy-by-design approach. We believe your personal information should remain private and under your control.

Data Collection and Processing

What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • No browsing history is collected or stored
  • No user accounts are required or created
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected
  • No personal information leaves your device

What We DO Process (Locally Only)

  • Text content you enter into supported LLM platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  • Detection patterns for PII/PHI identification
  • User preferences for detection sensitivity and redaction options

Local Storage Only

All data processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Detection algorithms run locally in your browser
  • User settings are stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • Redaction preferences are saved locally for your convenience
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used

How We Protect Your Privacy

Local-First Architecture

  • All PII/PHI detection happens on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers for analysis
  • Processing occurs in real-time as you type

Secure Data Handling

  • Temporary text analysis is performed in memory only
  • No persistent storage of your actual content
  • Settings and preferences use Chrome's secure storage APIs

Open Source Transparency

  • Our code is open source and available for review
  • You can verify exactly what the extension does
  • No hidden functionality or data collection

Supported Platforms

Privacy Guardian 2 works on these LLM platforms:

  • ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)
  • Claude (claude.ai)
  • Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)
  • Perplexity AI (perplexity.ai)
  • Microsoft Copilot (copilot.microsoft.com)

Permissions Explained

Required Permissions

  • Storage: To save your preferences locally
  • Active Tab: To analyze text on supported LLM websites
  • Scripting: To inject our privacy protection features
  • Alarms: For periodic updates to detection patterns

Host Permissions

We only request access to specific LLM platforms where the extension provides value. We do not have access to other websites you visit.

Your Rights and Controls

User Control

  • Enable/disable the extension at any time
  • Customize detection sensitivity and patterns
  • Choose redaction methods (masking, removal, pseudonymization)
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data

Compliance

Privacy Guardian 2 is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) - for PHI detection
  • Chrome Web Store privacy requirements

Changes to This Policy

  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
